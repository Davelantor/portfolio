About Me

I'm a product designer and UX lead with over a decade of experience building digital products that people actually want to use. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous user research, systems thinking, and high-craft visual design — across mobile, web, and enterprise platforms in some of Australia's most recognised companies.

What drives me is the space between complexity and clarity. I'm drawn to the hardest problems — onboarding flows that need to serve millions, design systems that need to scale across hundreds of components, service experiences that touch customers at their most stressed moments — and I find them satisfying precisely because solving them well takes every skill at once.

Outside of the screen, I think about how physical design and spatial reasoning inform digital work. I believe the best digital products feel like well-built objects: purposeful, considered, and satisfying to hold.

Quick Facts
10+ years across startup, scale-up, and enterprise
From 3-person founding teams to design orgs of 60+ people, I've worked at every scale and adapted accordingly — moving fast when needed, applying rigour when it counts.
Equally fluent in design and front-end code
I build prototypes in code when Figma isn't enough, contribute to component libraries in React, and can read a pull request without needing a translator. It changes how I collaborate with engineers.
Built design systems that outlasted my tenure
The systems I've established at Telstra and ANZ are still in active use. I treat design systems as product problems — with governance, contribution models, and adoption strategies — not just component libraries.
